Frequently Asked Questions

|Q.
If we come in before 10 and are not 21, can we stay?
|A.
No, the whole building becomes 21 and up after 10 and we will ask anyone under 21 to leave.
|Q. Can we bring in a cake and balloons.
|A. Yes, balloons and birthday desserts are allowed, but no other outside food or drinks are permitted.
|Q. When is happy hour?
|A. Our Social Hour is Monday - Friday from 4PM to 7PM and Monday, Wednesday and Thursday nights from 10PM to 2AM.
|Q. Do you take reservations?
|A. We do not take standard reservations, the only reservations we take are through contracted events.
|Q. What time does the kitchen close?
|A. The Kitchen closes at:
Sun - Thurs: 10 PM
Fri - Sat: 12 AM
